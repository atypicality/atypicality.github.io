Improving at Dance
Help with self-teaching?

+

If you are learning a dance or are planning to teach a dance, here are a few tips to help you and your students learn faster:

  1. Breakdown footwork and upper body separately, it helps a lot!
  2. Frequently record yourself to catch mistakes!
  3. Memorizing the song itself helps with cues and timing
  4. Pay attention to your facial expressions! If you are lost, it shows*
  5. Start a dance at 20-60% speed and work your way up to 100%
Need Dance Guides?

+

For beginners, a good idea is to practice common moves! For example:

  1. Body Rolls
  2. Head and Neck Isolations

Footwork (break-dancing footwork is semi-ubiquitous in all dances)

  1. Indian Step
  2. 2-step
  3. Toprocking
  4. Grapevine
  5. Shuffling
  6. Criss-cross

Handwork

  1. Figure 8
  2. Wrist Roll
  3. Locking
